How it works

Three steps to glory

Each player throws 6 pogs into the pot. Take turns slamming the stack — every coin that flips face-up is yours to keep. Empty the stack with the higher score and you win the loot.

1
🪙

Ante up

You and your opponent each toss 6 crypto pogs into a shared stack of 12. Real stakes, real bragging rights.

2
🎯

Charge & slam

Tap to start the power meter, tap again to lock it. The harder and cleaner your slam, the more pogs flip. Hit the gold zone for a PERFECT.

3
🏆

Keep the spoils

Flipped pogs go to your collection — and the winner pockets them for good. Climb your win/loss record and build the deck.

Timing is everything — stop the needle in the PERFECT zone for the biggest flips.
